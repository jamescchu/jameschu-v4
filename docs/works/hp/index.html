HP Knoq is the result of a HP Sponsored program at ArtCenter College of Design, and is a collaborative effort of 4 students from different majors.

At this time, presentation materials for this project is still in progress, there is a heck-of-a-lot of work to show and we want to do it right. However, to glimpse at our proud efforts, we have prepared a short video, showcasing our product and it's use-case in today's enviornments.

roles

As part of a 4 person team, I worked alongside Chloe Kim (Graphic Designer), Juno Park (Product Designer), and Yidan Zhang (Product Designer). This was fundamentally a heavily collaborative effort for us as we had to assume many different roles.

For my part, I worked on the internal designs for our weekly presentations, research and concept development, project management of the team, user interface designs, and assisted with the motion graphics in our video.

Special thanks to Nick Wang, who helped us film the video above, and Kevin who worked with me on the motion graphics and post editing that was involved.
