Process

Critr is a conceptual social media platform created and designed by myself. It is primarily focused on creating a community of designers that are sharing their work-in-progress photos or screenshot for critiques by others. This can help relieve creative blocks or the hassle of having to send over broken screenshots and pictures of your work to others and a lengthy explanation of your intentions, only to get it wrong.

This app is designed to foster a community of designers who can give and recieve feedback from comments that are "pinned" onto specific areas of an image.

home navigation

The home screen is split into 3 seperate views that are curated to deliver relavent content, as well as allowing discoverability for new users.

tab navigation

Navigating between tabs are straight forward and simple. A quick fade transition indicates that they are navigating on the same visual "plane".

project navigation

Projects transitions through a seperate "layer" to indicate the user is moving the project into view. This allows the user to visually comprehend where they are in the heigharchy without distracting their experience.

comment zoom

Seamlessly zoom into comments on the image to get better context. Double-tap or hit the back arrow to return to the full image.

prototype

The entire interaction was carefully crafted in Framer. Now that you've seen some of it's capabilities, feel free to play with it yourself, many small micro-interactions have been implemented to replicate a native application as much as possible.
